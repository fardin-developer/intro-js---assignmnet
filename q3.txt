



1. Unary Operators: It works with only one operand
let num = 10;
num++; 

2.Binary: It works with two operands
let a = 10;
let b = 5;
let sum = a + b; 

3. Conditional (Ternary) Operator:It works with three operand which is short way of
 writing if else.
 
let age = 20;
let isAdult = age >= 18 ? 'Yes' : 'No';
